Interesting facts About camel

click to make a camel

  1. Camels can reach 7 feet in height
  2. The word ‘camel’ comes from an Arabic word, when it’s literally
    translated, it means beauty. Don’t ever try and say that camels are ugly!
  3. There are more than 14 million camels in the world – most of them
    live in the Middle East, Asia and Australia.

Interesting facts About panda

click to make a panda

  1. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  2. Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their
    tummies for up to 12 hours, shifting up to 12 kilograms of bamboo!
  3. The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda
    melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot”.

Interesting facts About pigeon

click to make a pigeon

  1. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time
  2. Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks
    of 20-30 birds.
  3. Pigeons and humans have lived in close proximity for thousands of years.
    The first recordings of this date back to Mesopotamis, modern Iraq, in 3000bc.
